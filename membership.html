Membership

BECOME A MEMBER

The Marin Cyclists Club is dedicated to helping its members explore Marin County's beautiful natural areas. Many of our group rides pass by Muir Woods Redwood Forest, the Marin Headlands, Stinson Beach, Point Reyes National Seashore, and Mount Tamalpais (elevation 2,571 feet or 784 meters).

Benefits

  • Weekly organized bike rides for riders of all levels
  • Automatic email reminders (for those who don't opt out)
  • Free Marin Cyclists Club jersey when you lead four rides within a calendar year
  • Free bike maintenance clinics provided by local bike shops
  • Social events, including a fall picnic ride and a winter holiday party
  • 10% off at the Sports Basement in San Francisco, Sunshine Bikes in Fairfax, Whole Athlete bike-fitting services in San Anselmo, and Breakaway Bicycles in Ross
Membership dues are $20 per year per individual and are good for 12 months from the date of payment. If you register for the Marin Century, your annual dues are included in your registration fee.

RENEWAL

If you are already a member, and have received a notice to renew your membership, follow these simple instructions:

  • In the far lower left corner, enter your email and password, and click "Login"
  • Once logged in, in the same lower left corner, click on "View Profile."
  • Under "Membership details", click "Renew" button
  • Follow the instructions on the screen.
  • Choose “Pay by Paypal”. (You do not need your own pay pal account.)
  • PLEASE Read and Approve the Rider Waiver - If this is not approved, your membership will be placed on hold by the system.
If you have any problems along the way, please contact Info@MarinCyclists.com.

TROUBLESHOOTING

Invalid email address: Your membership is set up with the email address you provided. It may be incorrect. Please double check your address, and send your correct email address along with your name and address to marincyclistsclub@gmail.com.

No email address: Our club communicates through our website and email. If you do not have an email address, please visit the website for ride updates.

Waiver of liability: A copy of the waiver of liability can be read here. Please agree to the waiver when you register for membership.

Privacy information: The member directory is for current members only. You choose how much information to share with other current members. When you log in, select on "Edit Profile" to suppress information you don't wish to display. The information you allow to display can be edited by you alone, and only Marin Cyclists members can see it.
