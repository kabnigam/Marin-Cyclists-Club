The Marin Cyclists Club is dedicated to helping its members explore Marin County's beautiful natural areas. Many of our group rides pass by Muir Woods Redwood Forest, the Marin Headlands, Stinson Beach, Point Reyes National Seashore, and Mount Tamalpais (elevation 2,571 feet or 784 meters).
